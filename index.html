Our Management Features

Advanced tools and methodologies for effective wildlife population monitoring and conservation.

Population Tracking

Real-time monitoring of wildlife populations with trend analysis and predictive modeling for conservation planning.

Spatial Analysis

GIS mapping of wildlife movements, habitat use, and human-wildlife conflict hotspots for better management.

Camera Trapping

Automated image recognition from camera traps to identify and count individual animals across all parks.

Community Reporting

Integrated platform for local communities to report wildlife sightings and incidents in real-time.

Anti-Poaching

Advanced monitoring systems to detect and prevent poaching activities across protected areas.

Climate Impact

Analysis of climate change effects on wildlife habitats and predictive modeling for future scenarios.
